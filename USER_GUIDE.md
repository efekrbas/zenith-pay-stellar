# Zenith Pay User Guide

Welcome to Zenith Pay! This guide will walk you through the core features of our gasless payment platform built on the Stellar network.

## 🚀 Getting Started

### 1. Connect Your Wallet
- Ensure you have the [Freighter](https://www.stellar.org/freighter/) extension installed in your browser.
- Click the **"Connect Wallet"** button in the top navigation bar.
- Approve the connection request in the Freighter popup.

### 2. Fund Your Account (Testnet)
- If you are on the Stellar Testnet, use the [Stellar Laboratory](https://laboratory.stellar.org/#account-creator?network=testnet) to fund your account with test XLM.
- Zenith Pay uses the Stellar Testnet for all demonstrations.

## 💸 Making a Gasless Transfer

Zenith Pay allows you to send assets without paying network fees in XLM. We sponsor the fees for you!

1.  Navigate to the **Transfer** page.
2.  Enter the **Recipient Address** (starting with `G...`).
3.  Select the **Asset** you wish to send (XLM or any trusted asset).
4.  Enter the **Amount**.
5.  Click **"Confirm Transfer"**.
6.  Sign the transaction in your Freighter wallet.
7.  Wait for the animated status modal to confirm success!

## 📊 Viewing Metrics

Visit the **Metrics** page to see real-time analytics for the Zenith Pay network:
- **Transaction Volume**: Daily throughput of gasless transfers.
- **Active Users**: Growth of unique wallet addresses using the platform.
- **XLM Fees Saved**: Total impact of our fee sponsorship service.

## 📜 Transaction History

On the **Transfer** page, you can see a "Recent Activity" table.
- View your latest transactions.
- Click **"Details"** to view any transaction directly on the **Stellar Expert** explorer.
